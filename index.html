About Kerala

Explore the rich culture, heritage, and beauty of God's Own Country.

Kerala, also known as Keralam, is a state in India known for its backwaters, beaches, and hill stations. From the lush greenery of the Western Ghats to the serene backwaters of Alleppey, Kerala offers something for every traveler.
